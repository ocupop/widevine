Overview of Technology

Widevine provides digital media solutions for the delivery of digital entertainment to any device. Widevine’s DRM solution enables service providers to secure high quality video and audio.

4.2 Billion Devices
82 Billion Licenses
800 Partners

Today, millions of consumers enjoy digital entertainment secured and optimized by Widevine on retail consumer electronics devices from all major consumer brands.

Services using Widevine

Devices using Widevine

Brian Baker founded Widevine in 1999 to connect digital content to consumers. During his tenure as CEO, Widevine grew to become a global provider of video optimization and multiplatform content protection for telecom, cable, satellite, and internet video services. In December 2010, Google aquired Widevine (read Google’s post here). Widevine will continue to support its current customer base while enhancing both Google products and its own.
